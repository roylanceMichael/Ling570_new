A Republican House member says it's time for Democrats to stop the "Kabuki Dancing" surrounding the debt and deficit talks and that any debt deal should include a balanced budget amendment. 

"It's time for us to draw a line in the sand and say 'we're not going there' unless we have an enhanced balanced budget amendment...," Rep. Phil Gingrey, R-Ga., said to Shannon Bream on Tuesday's Power Play Live.
