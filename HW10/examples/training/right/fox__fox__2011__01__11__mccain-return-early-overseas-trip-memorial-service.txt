John McCain , the senior senator from Arizona, plans to cut short an overseas congressional fact-finding trip to attend the Wednesday memorial service for the shooting victims in Tucson. 

Spokeswoman Brooke Buchanan said the Republican senator and his wife, Cindy,will attend the 6 pm (local) service on the University of Arizona campus and a spokesperson for Arizona Republican Jon Kyl , says the state's junior senator will be there too. 

Rep. Raul Grijalva, D-Ariz., has said he plans to attend the service in Tucson as well. 

Rep. Jeff Flake, R-Ariz., will manage Republican time on the House floor Wednesday when the House considers a resolution honoring the dead and wounded and recognizing acts of heroism on Saturday. 

Fox News Senior House Producer Chad Pergram contributed to this report.
